## Maintainer Responsibilities

### All Maintainers

- Review and merge pull requests in their area of expertise
- Respond to issues within 48 hours
- Ensure code quality standards (see AGENTS.md)
- Maintain test coverage thresholds
- Keep documentation up to date
- Follow security best practices

### Tech Lead

- Approve smart contract changes
- Review architectural decisions
- Manage release process
- Coordinate with SCF on milestones
- Oversee security audits

### Project Lead (CEO)

- Final decision on project direction
- Anchor partnership negotiations
- Funding and grant management
- External communications
- Strategic planning

## Becoming a Maintainer

We welcome contributions from the community! To become a maintainer:

1. **Contribute consistently** - Submit quality PRs over time
2. **Help others** - Respond to issues, answer questions
3. **Understand the codebase** - Deep knowledge of at least one component
4. **Follow standards** - Adhere to AGENTS.md guidelines
5. **Get nominated** - Existing maintainer nomination + team vote
