## 7. DEPENDENCIES & RISKS

### 7.1 External Dependencies
| Dependency | Impact | Mitigation |
|------------|--------|------------|
| Stellar/Soroban development | High | Monitor SDF roadmap, engage with community |
| Anchor partnership availability | High | Maintain 3x pipeline of potential partners |
| Regulatory environment | Medium | Legal counsel + compliance flexibility |
| SCF grant approval | High | Alternative funding sources identified |

### 7.2 Risk Mitigation Timeline
| Quarter | Key Risks | Mitigation Actions |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| Q1 2026 | Anchor delays, smart contract bugs | Early LOIs, third-party audit |
| Q2 2026 | Low user adoption, fraud | Marketing budget, ML detection |
| Q3-Q4 2026 | Competition, scaling challenges | Differentiation, infrastructure investment |
| 2027 | Regulatory changes, market shifts | Compliance team, market diversification |
